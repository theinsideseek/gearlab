This Privacy Policy explains how Gear Lab collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit this site.

1. Affiliate Disclosure

Gear Lab participates in affiliate programs. Some links on this site are affiliate links, and we may earn a commission if you make a purchase through those links, at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate relationships do not control our editorial opinions, rankings, or recommendations.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Basic technical data (IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, timestamps).
  • Referral and attribution data (for example, which link or campaign led you to the site).
  • Information you voluntarily provide (for example, if you contact us by email).

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We and third parties may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies for:

  • Essential site functionality.
  • Analytics and performance measurement.
  • Affiliate link attribution and conversion tracking.
  • Fraud prevention and security.

You can manage cookies in your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect site functionality.

4. How We Use Data

We may use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the site.
  • Analyze traffic and content performance.
  • Measure affiliate campaign effectiveness.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or security incidents.
  • Respond to inquiries and legal obligations.

5. Sharing of Data

We may share limited information with:

  • Analytics providers.
  • Affiliate networks and merchant partners.
  • Hosting, CDN, and security service providers.
  • Authorities when required by law.

We do not share personal data beyond what is reasonably necessary for these purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for business, legal, security, or reporting purposes, then delete or anonymize it where practical.

7. Data Safety and Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including access controls and secure transport (HTTPS), to protect information.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data, and to object to certain processing.

To make a privacy request, contact us at: privacy@gearlab.example

9. Third-Party Links

This site may link to external websites (including merchants and affiliate partners). We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

10. Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
